# Project Overview

## Vision
To redefine digital gifting by adding anticipation, surprise, and decentralized trust.

## Strategic Goals
- **Anticipation by Design**: Use technology to recreate the feeling of a physical "wrapped" gift in a digital space.
- **Trustless Custody**: Ensure senedrs and recipients that funds are safe and cannot be accessed early.
- **Stellar Utility**: Showcase the real-world application of Soroban smart contracts for time-sensitive financial interactions.
